Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and answers about contacting and working with me

Research Collaboration

Q: How can I propose a research collaboration?

A: Send an email with your research proposal, objectives, and timeline. Include your background and how our expertise can complement each other.

Q: What types of research projects do you work on?

A: I focus on energy systems modeling, renewable energy, green hydrogen, energy security, and sustainable development for developing countries.

Consulting Services

Q: What consulting services do you offer?

A: I provide consulting in energy systems modeling, renewable energy policy, green hydrogen development, and sustainable energy solutions.

Q: How do you handle consulting projects?

A: Projects typically start with an initial consultation to understand needs, followed by a detailed proposal and structured implementation approach.

Academic Inquiries

Q: Can I request a copy of your publications?

A: Yes, please send an email with the specific publication title and your intended use. I'm happy to share research papers when appropriate.

Q: Do you supervise graduate students?

A: Yes, I supervise undergraduate and graduate students. Please contact me to discuss potential research topics and supervision opportunities.

General Questions

Q: What's the best way to contact you?

A: Email is preferred for most inquiries. Use the contact form for detailed messages, or call during office hours for urgent matters.

Q: How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

A: I typically respond to emails within 24-48 hours. For urgent matters, please call during office hours.
