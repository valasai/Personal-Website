Published Books

My books share experiences and knowledge with a broader audience, from travelogues to academic insights

In addition to my extensive research publications, I have also authored books that share my experiences and knowledge with a broader audience. My travelogue "سفر واشنگٽن جو" (Journey to Washington) provides insights into international academic travel and serves as a guide for students and professionals embarking on similar journeys.

This book represents a unique contribution to Sindhi literature as it's the first travelogue written by a student about their international academic journey. It offers practical guidance for those planning to travel abroad for academic purposes, including tips on cost-effective travel, cultural interactions, and making the most of international experiences.

Journey to Washington

2020 Sindhi سنڌ سلامت ڪتاب گهر

This travelogue chronicles my journey to Washington, D.C. for an international conference where I presented my research paper. The book serves as a comprehensive guide for students and professionals planning to travel to the United States for academic purposes.

Written in Sindhi language, this book is unique as it's the first travelogue by a student describing their international academic journey. It provides practical insights into cost-effective travel, cultural interactions, and making the most of international academic experiences.

Writing Philosophy

My writing philosophy centers on making knowledge accessible to diverse audiences. Whether writing academic papers or books, I believe in clear communication that bridges the gap between complex research and practical understanding.

My travelogue "سفر واشنگٽن جو" exemplifies this philosophy by translating my international academic experiences into practical guidance for students. I aim to share knowledge that can benefit others in their academic and professional journeys.

I believe that academic research should have practical applications and contribute to solving real-world problems. My publications reflect this commitment to bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical implementation in the energy sector.
