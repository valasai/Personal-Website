Research Overview

My research focuses on developing integrated modeling frameworks for sustainable energy transitions, with particular emphasis on the intersection of climate, energy, water, and food systems (CLEWS). I employ advanced optimization techniques, artificial intelligence, and system dynamics modeling to address critical challenges in energy planning, renewable energy integration, green hydrogen economics, and energy equity.

My work spans multiple scales—from community-level microgrids to national energy systems and international energy corridors. I collaborate with institutions across Pakistan, Turkey, Australia, Saudi Arabia, the United States, and Europe, maintaining active research profiles on ResearchGate with over 110,615 reads and 1,654 citations. The ultimate goal is to provide evidence-based, implementable solutions that enable developing and developed nations to achieve climate commitments while ensuring energy security, economic development, and social equity.

Research Focus Areas

Hydrogen Energy Modelling

Development of comprehensive models for hydrogen production, storage, transportation, and international trade corridors, including techno-economic analysis and policy frameworks for hydrogen deployment.

Tools: OSeMOSYS, Pyomo, Julia/JuMP, Gurobi, GLPK

Energy Systems Modeling

Comprehensive modeling of energy systems using advanced optimization techniques, including capacity expansion planning, dispatch optimization, and integrated resource planning for sustainable energy transitions.

Tools: TIMES, LEAP, OSeMOSYS, Pyomo, Gurobi, PLEXOS

Low Carbon Development

Research on pathways for low-carbon energy transitions, including renewable energy integration, carbon reduction strategies, and sustainable development policies for emerging economies.

Tools: LEAP, TIMES, Policy Analysis Models, Carbon Footprint Assessment

Water-Energy Nexus

Integrated modeling of water and energy systems, addressing the complex interdependencies between water resources and energy production, including thermal power plant water requirements and renewable energy water impacts.

Tools: System Dynamics, Pyomo, Gurobi, Water-Energy Models

Energy Economics

Economic analysis of energy systems, including cost-benefit analysis, market modeling, pricing mechanisms, and economic impact assessment of energy policies and renewable energy investments.

Tools: Economic Models, Cost Analysis, Market Simulation, Policy Impact Assessment

Smart Grid Modeling

Advanced modeling of smart grid technologies, including distributed energy resources, demand response, energy storage systems, and grid modernization strategies for enhanced reliability and efficiency.

Tools: Grid Simulation, Power System Analysis, Optimization Algorithms, Smart Grid Technologies
