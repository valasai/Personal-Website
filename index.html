About My Work

I am an Associate Professor of Mechanical Engineering at Quaid-e-Awam University of Engineering, Science & Technology (QUEST) in Pakistan. My research focuses on energy modeling, economics, security, policy, and management with a particular emphasis on low-carbon development pathways using advanced tools like AnswerTIMES (MARKAL) and LEAP Energy Model.

My work centers on low carbon development pathways, water constraints in future power generation, demand-side modeling of the power sector, and techno-economic feasibility modeling of smart grids. I maintain an active research profile with 47 publications, over 109,000 reads, and 1,576 citations on ResearchGate.

Beyond research, I am passionate about teaching and mentoring students. I currently teach Renewable Energy and Emerging Technologies and have previously taught courses including Power Plants, Pollution Analysis and Control, Renewable Energy Resources, and Tribology. I hold a PhD in Energy Modelling from Mehran University of Engineering and Technology (MUET), Jamshoro.
